About Me

Hello and welcome to my professional website and blog! My name is Quinn Rafferty (she/her/hers) and I love statistics, data, and health. I recently obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Applied Mathematics & Statistics with a concentration in Community and Global Health from Macalester College in St. Paul, Minnesota. This upcoming September I will join the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation as a Post Bachelor Fellow (PBF) in Seattle, Washington. Through the IHME, I plan to pursue my Master’s of Science degree in Health Statistics and Evaluation at the University of Washington.
